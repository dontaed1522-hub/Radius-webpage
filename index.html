"Cross Hearts Communities Nations"

The Great Commission, Jesus says, “Go therefore and make disciples of all nations… teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you” (Matthew 28:19–20). The cross is the foundation of our message, the gospel of Christ that we proclaim. We allow God to change our hearts reflects as we learn to obey Christ. We used changed hearts to make disciples in our communities together, sending them outward to nations.

Gospel

When Adam & Eve ate of the forbidden fruit, all of humanity was seperated from God by sin. The wage of the sin we all carry... is Death. But, by the glory of God who sent down his son to live a PERFECT LIFE and die on a cross, we have the gift of his GRACE and MERCY. Jesus loved us so much that he made the ultimate sacrifice, so we could reckognize our sin, repent and ask for Forgivness, and be REDEEMED into the righteousness of Christ
