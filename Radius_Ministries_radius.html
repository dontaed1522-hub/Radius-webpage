Our Mission

Our mission is to share the gospel of Jesus Christ by building a community centered on the cross, where hearts are seen, lives are transformed, and people are sent to impact communities and nations. We believe church is not a place, but a people united in faith and purpose.

Our story

Radius was initialy started around 2020 during the pandemic. with churches closed and lockdown imposed, The Johnston family hosted bible studioes at their house for their childrens and friends. soon, their numbers grew, friends invited friend and studies grew in depth. As the group expanded, they took the initiative to do cherity work with mission organizations. eventually became closely associated with, and supported by Salt and Light Church, a group who share not only faith in christ, but a love for scripture and faithful labor. Radius still activly holds bible studies and serves its community, but as old members push forward, we grow with anticpation for the next chapter in Gods plan for us.

Salt & Light Church began on February 24, 2002 as Marion & Brenda Zeigler followed what they believed God was calling them to do- begin a church in the Sumter community that would effectively reach lost people with the gospel of Jesus Christ and help them become devoted disciples of Jesus Christ.

We will become a strategic center for sharing Christ with the world. This will be accomplished one person at a time, becoming followers of the teachings and lifestyle of Jesus Christ.
